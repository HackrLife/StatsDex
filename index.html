About Statsdex

Statsdex is a data storytelling platform built by HackrLife Media LLC. Player profiles, match reviews, coaching analysis — each story combines verified Sofascore data with Statsdex proprietary metrics: Game Impact Score, Big Game Index, Consistency Score.

About BarcaFutbol

BarcaFutbol is the flagship football editorial property of HackrLife Media LLC. Published on Beehiiv with 4,000+ subscribers. Data-first, narrative-led. The analytical voice in the gap between The Athletic and StatsBomb.
