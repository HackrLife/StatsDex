About Statsdex

Statsdex is a data storytelling platform built by HackrLife Media LLC. We produce scroll-driven interactive data stories across football, and soon across travel, culture, finance and more. Every story combines established analytics with Statsdex proprietary metrics — Game Impact Score, Big Game Index, Consistency Score — designed to surface what standard numbers miss.

About BarcaFutbol

BarcaFutbol is the flagship football editorial property of HackrLife Media LLC. Published on Beehiiv with 4,000+ subscribers, it covers FC Barcelona and the elite player ecosystem around them. Data-first, narrative-led. The analytical voice in the gap between The Athletic and StatsBomb.
